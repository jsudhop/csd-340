Wacipi (pow wow) - celebrate culture through food, dance, traditional practices. Leah and her grandmother typically hand sew their ribbon shirts and jingle dresses. Leah always wears a jingle dress and participates in the opening dances.

Leah participates in annual archery competitions at the wacipi's and practices year round.

Leah enjoys all kinds of physical activity, in addition to axe throwing and archery she also practices jiu-jitsu.
