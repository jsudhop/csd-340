"Fever Dreams"

Chaos screaming in silent illusions,
Forbidden dreams mating with tempting nightmares.
Eye and mind's dangerous elusion,
Self-trickery is the most tastefully rotten affair.

Pandemonium caressing the edge of equanimity,
As reality quakes in decadent tribulation.
The euphoric presence of corruptions proximity...
A cursed blessing dazed by broken minds sedation.

Screams of the familiar vibrate in the air,
And nails crack in the marring of perfect flesh.
Onlookers judge a mind in disrepair,
As, the leaking of sanity and fertile beginnings mesh.

Such is the craving of mania,
The welcome daze of delusion.
Fevered fantasy the tankard of dipsomania,
Sipping on melancholia's poison infusion.

Shadows taunting from nirvana's edge,
Sardonic angelics gormandize on absolution,
Blackened innocence they retch,
Showering in tainted prosecutions.

Judge, Jury, and Executioner endogenous.
Fleeing in voids of shifting aura,
Wailing psyche blames it on them or us.
Chains bind denials plea, your pandora.

A pinch to steal your beguiling horror,
MD the insignia of heavens inimical subjugators.
"Perhaps some more." Bribes sedation's adorer.
Chemical tethers veil phobia's renovator.

Your pandora.
Smother it, welcome silence.
Too revolted, too nescient to face psychosis's chimera.
Resolve in carved scars on the inside for iniquitous balance.

"Siege on Psyche"

Will you barter death for peace?
Carve a path of the guilty's flesh,
Their trophy on the mantelpiece?
Leave a trail of the innocent's blood,
Like sacrificial chess pieces?
Greed veiled by Justice's golden headpiece.
Armor forged in stolen benevolence.
Words a weapon never to cease,
Blows traded in merciful masks of the deceitful.
Manufacturing your strangling prison of felo-de-se.
So you traded death for peace,
Staring at Tophet buried six feet down,
Put to rest with regrets offered as a feast,
Blanketed by the grit of a broken will,
Never enough golden moments to fill Trauma's creases.
